Lazy Broccoli

October 11, 2017

Sorry for not posting lately! Work has been overwhelming me - I have an ACT test coming up soon.

A few weeks ago, I was taking a break and looking at my garden. I was appreciating my plants when I saw a welcome sight.

I was so excited that I quickly snapped a photo and shared it on my social media (follow me at instagram.com/erik.zhang). I quickly got wrapped up on my phone and soon left the scene.

Unfortunately, I forgot about the broccoli. And when I looked at it again two weeks later, my broccoli was so overgrown that I was forced to cut it all down to keep it from overwhelming the other plants. I can't get lazy next time, and I need to pay better attention to details.
